AYSA Kent Academy Website

A comprehensive web platform for youth soccer academy

Project Overview

Timeline

4 Weeks

My Role

UX Research, UI Design, Visual Design, Prototyping

Tools Used

Figma, Adobe XD, React, Node.js

Team

4 UX Designers

As part of a team at the University of Washington, we designed and developed a comprehensive website for AYSA Kent Academy, a youth soccer organization. The project focused on creating an engaging online platform that streamlines team management, improves communication between stakeholders, and provides real-time updates about games and training sessions.

The Challenge

AYSA Kent Academy needed a modern, user-friendly website that would serve as a central hub for players, parents, and coaches. The existing system relied heavily on manual processes and lacked a centralized platform for communication and team management.

Problem Statement

How might we create a digital platform that streamlines communication and team management for AYSA Kent Academy while providing an engaging experience for all stakeholders?

Project Goals

  • Create a centralized platform for team management and communication
  • Streamline scheduling and attendance tracking
  • Improve the registration and payment process
  • Provide real-time updates and notifications

Design Process

1. Research

Competitive Analysis

Analyzed 4 competitor websites to identify industry standards and opportunities for innovation.

Key Findings
  • Most platforms lack real-time communication features
  • Mobile responsiveness is often overlooked
  • Complex navigation structures create user friction

User Interviews

Conducted 12 interviews with stakeholders to understand their needs and pain points.

"I need a way to quickly check my child's practice schedule and communicate with coaches without switching between multiple apps." - Parent of Youth Player
Key Insights
  • Parents need centralized schedule management
  • Coaches want streamlined attendance tracking
  • Players desire easy access to training materials

Final Solution

The final product delivers a comprehensive platform that addresses all stakeholder needs while maintaining an intuitive and engaging user experience.

Key Features

Team Management

Comprehensive tools for managing rosters, schedules, and communications.

Parent Portal

Dedicated space for parents to track progress and communicate.

Mobile Experience

Responsive design for seamless access on any device.

Project Impact

  • 50% reduction in administrative time
  • 90% parent engagement rate
  • 95% user satisfaction score

Lessons Learned

This project reinforced the importance of thorough user research and iterative design. Key takeaways include:

  • Early stakeholder involvement leads to better adoption
  • Mobile-first design is crucial for sports-related applications
  • Regular user testing helps identify and address pain points early
