TEAM PROJECT UI/UX DESIGN WEB DEVELOPMENT

Project Overview

Designed and developed a modern business website for a future boba tea shop. The project focused on creating an engaging online presence that showcases the brand's unique identity and menu offerings.

Design Process

Created a vibrant and inviting design that reflects the fun and energetic atmosphere of a boba tea shop. Focused on creating an intuitive menu navigation and engaging visual elements.

Responsive Design

Optimized for all devices and screen sizes
