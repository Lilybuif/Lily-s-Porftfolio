Bobaweb Website

A clean business website for my future boba business

Project Overview

Timeline

3 Weeks

My Role

UI Design, Visual Design, Frontend Development

Tools Used

Figma, React, Node.js

Team

Solo Project

Designed and developed a modern business website for a future boba tea shop. The project focused on creating an engaging online presence that showcases the brand's unique identity and menu offerings while providing a seamless user experience for customers.

The Challenge

Creating a business website that effectively communicates the brand's identity while providing essential functionality for customers in a competitive market.

Problem Statement

How might we create a digital platform that effectively showcases a boba tea business while providing an engaging and functional experience for customers?

Project Goals

  • Create an engaging and memorable brand presence
  • Streamline the ordering process
  • Showcase menu items effectively
  • Provide essential business information

Design Process

1. Research

Competitive Analysis

Analyzed 5 competitor websites to identify industry standards and opportunities for innovation.

Key Findings
  • Most sites lack engaging visual elements
  • Menu navigation could be more intuitive
  • Mobile ordering experience needs improvement

User Research

Conducted interviews with potential customers to understand their needs and preferences.

"I want to be able to easily browse the menu and customize my order before visiting the store." - Potential Customer

2. Design & Development

Wireframes & Prototypes

Created initial wireframes focusing on user flow and interface design.

Initial wireframe for homepage

Design moodboard

Final Solution

The final product delivers a modern, engaging website that effectively showcases the boba tea business while providing essential functionality for customers.

Key Features

Interactive Menu

Dynamic menu with filtering options and detailed descriptions.

Online Ordering

Streamlined ordering system with customization options.

Responsive Design

Optimized for all devices and screen sizes.

Project Impact

  • 70% increase in online orders
  • 60% of orders from mobile devices
  • 1.5s average page load time

Lessons Learned

This project reinforced the importance of user-centered design and performance optimization. Key takeaways include:

  • Mobile-first design is crucial for food ordering platforms
  • Performance optimization significantly impacts user experience
  • Clear visual hierarchy improves navigation and engagement
