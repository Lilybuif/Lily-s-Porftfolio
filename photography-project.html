Photography Website for Friends

A clean portfolio site with custom logos and gallery for a couple's photo blog

TEAM PROJECT UI/UX DESIGN WEB DEVELOPMENT

Project Overview

Designed a clean portfolio site with custom logos and gallery for a couple's photo blog. The project focused on creating an elegant and user-friendly platform to showcase their photography work and share their journey.

Design Process

Collaborated with the couple to understand their vision and photography style. Created custom logos and designed a gallery layout that highlights their work while maintaining a clean, professional aesthetic.

Key Features

Custom Logo Design

Unique branding that reflects the couple's photography style

Photo Gallery

Elegant gallery layout with smooth transitions

Blog Integration

Easy-to-use blog section for sharing stories

Responsive Design

Optimized viewing experience across all devices
