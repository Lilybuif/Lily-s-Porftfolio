Photography Website for Friends

A clean portfolio site with custom logos and gallery for a couple's photo blog

Project Overview

Timeline

4 Weeks

My Role

UI Design, Visual Design, Frontend Development

Tools Used

Figma, React, Cloudinary

Team

Collaboration with Photographers

Designed a clean portfolio site with custom logos and gallery for a couple's photo blog. The project focused on creating an elegant and user-friendly platform to showcase their photography work and share their journey while maintaining a professional aesthetic.

The Challenge

Creating a photography portfolio that effectively showcases the couple's work while maintaining a clean, professional aesthetic and providing an engaging user experience.

Problem Statement

How might we create a digital platform that effectively showcases a couple's photography work while providing an engaging and professional experience for viewers?

Project Goals

  • Create a unique brand identity through custom logo design
  • Showcase photography work in an elegant gallery
  • Provide an engaging blog experience
  • Ensure optimal image loading and performance

Design Process

1. Research

Competitive Analysis

Analyzed 6 photography portfolio websites to identify best practices and opportunities for innovation.

Key Findings
  • Most portfolios focus on visual impact
  • Gallery navigation varies significantly
  • Image loading optimization is crucial

User Research

Conducted interviews with the couple to understand their vision and photography style.

"We want our website to reflect our unique style while making it easy for visitors to explore our work." - Photography Couple

2. Design & Development

Wireframes & Prototypes

Created initial wireframes focusing on gallery layout and user experience.

Initial wireframe for gallery

Design moodboard

Final Solution

The final product delivers an elegant portfolio website that effectively showcases the couple's photography work while providing an engaging user experience.

Key Features

Custom Logo Design

Unique branding that reflects the couple's photography style.

Photo Gallery

Elegant gallery layout with smooth transitions and optimized loading.

Blog Integration

Easy-to-use blog section for sharing stories and updates.

Project Impact

  • 50% increase in photo views
  • 40% mobile traffic
  • 2s average page load time

Lessons Learned

This project reinforced the importance of visual design and performance optimization. Key takeaways include:

  • Image optimization is crucial for photography websites
  • Custom branding enhances professional presence
  • Responsive design is essential for photo viewing
